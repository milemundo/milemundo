About Hoover Dam

Hoover Dam is one of the most famous engineering landmarks in the American Southwest and one of the top day-trip experiences from Las Vegas.

A Legendary Landmark

Located between Nevada and Arizona, Hoover Dam stands as one of the most recognized symbols of modern engineering in the desert. Its scale, surrounding cliffs, and panoramic views make it an unforgettable stop for visitors exploring the region.

Views, History & Experience

Visitors love Hoover Dam not only for its history, but also for the dramatic desert scenery, the Colorado River views, nearby overlooks, and the chance to combine the visit with other custom experiences around Las Vegas.

Scenic Desert Setting
